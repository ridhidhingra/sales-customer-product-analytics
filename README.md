Sales, Product & Customer Analysis
-) Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales, product, and customer data using SQL to generate actionable business insights. It simulates a real-world retail database environment and demonstrates how SQL can be used for performance analysis, reporting, and customer segmentation.

-) Key Analysis Performed

Identified first and last order dates and overall business time range

Calculated total sales, total orders, items sold, and average selling price

Generated a business KPI summary report using UNION ALL

Analyzed yearly and monthly sales trends, including cumulative sales

Evaluated product performance by comparing yearly sales with average and previous-year sales

Determined top revenue-contributing products and their percentage contribution

Segmented customers into VIP, Regular, and New based on spending behavior and lifespan

Created a detailed customer business report including age groups and purchase patterns
